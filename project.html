    Healthy Beacon | App | 2021

    Introduction

    Healthy Beacon is a health-oriented mobile application. It aims to lead people to achieve a healthy lifestyle using the theory of gamification.

    Function

    There are 5 major functions in this prototype, such as "BMI & Calories Calculator", "Pedometer", "E-Pet", "Fitness Planner" and "Meal Planner". Users are motivated to exercise and record their walking steps, which will be converted to E-pet food. User can also evaluate and enhance their sports performance using the remaining functions.
